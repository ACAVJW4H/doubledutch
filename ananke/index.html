Ananke

A CRUD app for Clotho 3.0

Ananke is an open source CRUD app for Clotho 3.0. The main purpose of this tool is demonstrate basic Create, Read, Update and Delete operations in Clotho.

Why call it Ananke?

In greek mythology, Ananke (/əˈnæŋkiː/) was the personification of destiny and fate. She was often depicted by holding the spindle. She was said to be the only deity who had control over the decisions of the Morai: Clotho, Lachesis and Atropos. One version even considers Ananke has as the mother of the 3 sisters.
